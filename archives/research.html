My research is on the interplay between information and stochastic control theory specifically on

¾       QoS analysis and reliability of feedback communication systems

¾       Inverse optimal control for two-agent team decision problems.

Other interests include sequential prediction and learning algorithms, specifically in the context of online ad auctions and algorithmic trading.
