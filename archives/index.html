Biography : I am a graduate student in the Electrical Engineering Dept. at the University of Illinois, Urbana Champaign. I received my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, India in 2007 and M.S in ECE and M.S in Statistics both from UIUC in 2009 and 2011 respectively. I was a visiting-researcher at the Research Laboratory for Electronics at MIT in the summer of 2009 and a Research Intern at Adchemy Inc. in the summer of 2010. I have also been awarded the James Henderson fellowship at UIUC and S Subramanian Award (Institute Merit Prize) from IIT Madras in the years 2008 and 2004 respectively.


My research interests are broadly in the areas of stochastic control, information theory, statistical learning and prediction. Please see my publications for specific interests.
